Fun Cat Facts

Think you know everything about cats? From their mysterious purrs to their quirky whiskers, our feline friends are full of surprises. Here are some paw-sitively fascinating facts that will make you appreciate your kitty even more!

10 Fun Cat Facts You Might Not Know

  1. Cats sleep… a lot. On average, cats snooze between 13–16 hours a day. That’s nearly two-thirds of their life!
  2. Their purr is powerful. A cat’s purr can calm them down—but studies suggest it might also help lower stress and blood pressure in humans.
  3. Whiskers are sensors. Whiskers aren’t just cute—they help cats measure spaces and sense changes in their environment.
  4. They land (almost) on their feet. Cats have a flexible backbone and a special “righting reflex” that lets them twist mid-air when falling.
  5. Meowing is just for us. Cats rarely “meow” to each other—it’s a behavior they mostly use to communicate with humans.
  6. Cats have super hearing. They can detect sounds at frequencies up to 64,000 Hz—almost twice what a dog can hear.
  7. Big jumpers. A healthy cat can leap up to six times their body length in a single bound.
  8. Every nose is unique. Just like human fingerprints, each cat’s nose print is one-of-a-kind.
  9. Old souls. The oldest known pet cat lived to be 38 years old! (The average housecat lives 12–18 years.)
  10. Ancient companions. Cats were domesticated around 10,000 years ago and were once worshipped in Ancient Egypt.
